Mental health in the juvenile justice system

By Hailey Peck | April 22, 2024

Mental Health has become a growing concern over the past few years across the board. As it continues to become more prevlent, it is important to consider all aspects. One that is often overlooked is how it impacts those in the juvenile justice system.

Juveniles entering the system

70% of all juveniles that enter the justice system, enter with pre-diagnosed mental health issues.

Services and Stays

There are two tests that every juvenile is given upon entrance to the justice system. From there stays can range from one day to more than two years.

Diagnosed mental health vs treatment

As the trend of those entering the juvenile justice system with pre-diagnosed mental health issues increases, so does the numebr of juveniles that do not recieve treatment.
